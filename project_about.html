Hello everyone, my name is Olena (Helen). I have been working as a designer for over 10 years. I have always dreamed of creating unusual jewelry and interior decors with natural motifs. I am inspired by the nature of my Ukraine. I was born in the south, in a beautiful land of steppes and seas. I dedicate a lot of my jewelry and decorations to the South. I found inspiration for mushroom jewelry in the forests of the Vinnitsa region and the Carpathians. Real magic lives in these forests. You can be sure that the magic of the forest lives in mushroom jewelry from MyAirFlower shop :)
